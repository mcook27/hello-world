Customcraft Gates

Customcraft Gates Pty. Ltd. provides total project management, from conception to completion.

We are manufacturers of modern custom-designed steel gates (sliding and swing) and also of matching fence panels. Customcraft Gates install gate automation and intercom systems, including all associated electrical work. All products are protected by quality galvanising and powder coating.

Customers will receive personal service. Your project will be measured, manufactured and installed by the company owner.

Customcraft Gates is located in the south-eastern suburbs of Melbourne. Work has been completed in Brighton, Hampton, Glen Iris, Malvern and the Mornington Peninsula. We are happy to negotiate projects in other suburbs and country Victoria.

Customcraft Gates PTY LTD began in 1990 building on previous experience in metal fabrication since 1979. We are a family-run business that aims to provide quality products and excellent service.
